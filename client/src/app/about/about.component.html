FAQ

Find the answers to the most frequently asked questions below.

How does Matbakhi find the best prices?

Matbakhi uses real-time data from partner supermarkets and other sources to compare prices and ensure you get the best deals for your ingredients.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Absolutely! You can create an account to save your favorite recipes and access them anytime.

Are the listed supermarket prices always up to date?

We strive to keep all prices accurate and up to date by syncing with supermarket data frequently. However, there may occasionally be slight delays in updates.

Can I filter recipes by dietary preferences?

Yes! Matbakhi allows you to filter recipes by dietary preferences such as vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and more.

Is Matbakhi free to use?

Yes! Matbakhi offers free access to its core features. Premium features, if available, will be clearly marked.

Can I create a shopping list directly on Matbakhi?

Of course! You can generate a shopping list based on your selected recipes and even organize it by supermarket for convenience.

Does Matbakhi include international recipes?

Yes! Matbakhi features a diverse collection of recipes from different cuisines around the world.

How do I pay for ingredients from partner supermarkets?

Payments are made directly through the partner supermarket’s website or in-store. Matbakhi does not process transactions.

What if I can't find a specific recipe or ingredient?

If you can't find something, feel free to use our "Request a Recipe" feature or suggest new ingredients to our team.
